Emission line luminosity functions

In this webpage, you can find all the data analysis and corresponding code that was used in the paper : The evolution of the [OII], Hbeta and [OIII] emission-line luminosity functions from redshift 0.2 to 1.4 with 36,000 spectra by Johan Comparat et al.

You can browse through these folders to retrieve the observed LFs or the fitting functions.
