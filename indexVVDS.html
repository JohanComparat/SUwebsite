VIMOS VLT Deep Survey data folder
    The publication Le Fevre et al. 2013 explains in details the observations made during the VIMOS VLT Deep Survey (WIDE, DEEP and UDEEP).
    Content
    catalogs folder containing the catalogs of the WIDE, DEEP and UDEEP. The "summary" catalogs contain the photometry and the redshifts. The "linesFitted" catalog contains the information of line fits. The "LFcatalog" contains additionally the line luminosity in the Om=0.3 H=70. cosmology.
    photometry folder containing the photometric catalogs of the WIDE, DEEP and UDEEP. Currently empty.
    products folder containing the value-added products : emission line luminosity functions and spectral stacks.
    spectra folder containing all released spectra of the WIDE, DEEP and UDEEP.
