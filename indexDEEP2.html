DEEP2 survey data folder
    The publication Newman et al. 2013 explains in details the observations made during the DEEP2 survey.
    Content
    catalogs folder containing the DEEP2 catalog. The "summary" catalogs contain the photometry and the redshifts. The "linesFitted" catalog contains the information of line fits . The "LFcatalog" contains additionally the line luminosity in the Om=0.3 H=70. cosmology.
    photometry folder containing the photometric catalogs. Currently empty.
    products folder containing the value-added products : emission line luminosity functions and spectral stacks.
    spectra folder containing all released spectra of DEEP2 in count and flux calibrated.
