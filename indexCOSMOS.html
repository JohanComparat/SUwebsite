Emission line galaxies observed on COSMOS

We observed about 2200 emission line galaxy spectra on the COSMOS field with the VLT FORS2 instrument as a base to estimate the [OII] luminosity function at redshift greater than 1.

For more details on the [OII] LF

Please read the paper : The 0.1 < z < 1.65 evolution of the bright end of the [OII] luminosity function by Johan Comparat, Johan Richard, Jean-Paul Kneib, Olivier Ilbert, V.
