VIMOS Public Extragalactic Redshift Survey
    The publication Guzzo, L. et al. 2014 explains in details the observations made during the VIPERS survey.
    Content
    catalogs folder containing the catalogs. The "summary" catalogs contain the photometry and the redshifts. The "linesFitted" catalog contains the information of line fits. The "LFcatalog" contains additionally the line luminosity in the Planck 2015 cosmology.
    photometry folder containing the photometric catalogs.
    products folder containing the value-added products : emission line luminosity functions and spectral stacks.
    spectra folder containing all released spectra.
